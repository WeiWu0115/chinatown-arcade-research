# Interview Guide: Chinatown ARcade Post-Experience Interview

Semi-structured interview, conducted immediately after participants completed the AR experience.

---

## Core Questions (asked in all/most interviews)

| # | Question | Purpose |
|---|----------|---------|
| Q1 | Can you tell us who you are and what you do? | Participant background; establish rapport |
| Q2 | How was the overall experience for you? | Open-ended first impression; unprimed reaction |
| Q3 | What are the good things and the bad things? Can you be more specific — what do you like and what don't you like? | Specific positive/negative feedback on gameplay |
| Q4 | Any hiccups? | Technical issues, bugs, confusion points |
| Q5 | Anything else you think we should improve? | Open improvement suggestions |
| Q6 | If we were to charge for this experience, how much do you think we should charge? | Willingness to pay |
| Q7 | Any last words for our project, or for Chinatown? | Closing; emotional/cultural reflection |

## Supplementary Questions (asked selectively based on context)

| # | Question | When Asked | Purpose |
|---|----------|-----------|---------|
| Q8 | What kind of souvenir would you like to bring home after this experience? | Rounds 3-4 (Nov 2025) | Merchandise strategy |
| Q9 | What about blind boxes? Are you interested in LaBooBoo-style blind boxes? | Follow-up to Q8 | Specific product validation |
| Q10 | Do you feel comfortable wearing the glasses? Is it heavy? | When participant showed discomfort | Hardware comfort |
| Q11 | How long did it take you to finish the whole experience? | Select interviews | Duration benchmarking |
| Q12 | How far do you think we are in terms of really monetizing from this experience? | Asked to XR industry experts (P10) | Expert commercial assessment |
| Q13 | What do you think our project means to Chinatown? | Asked to local community members (P17) | Cultural significance |
| Q14 | Do you feel you can navigate the experience without any guide? | Asked to P16 | Onboarding self-sufficiency |
| Q15 | What are your first impressions of this poster? Does it match the experience? | Asked to P14 (with poster shown) | Marketing material feedback |

## Interview Logistics

- **Format**: Semi-structured; core questions followed in approximate order, with natural follow-ups
- **Timing**: Immediately post-experience, on-site in Chinatown
- **Duration**: 3-15 minutes per participant (varies by depth of response)
- **Recording**: Video (DJI camera + phone)
- **Setting**: Street-side, informal; some interviews conducted with group participants together
- **Interviewer**: Team members (primarily Zihao Zhang)
- **Language**: English (one interview, P14/Kefan, conducted partly in Chinese)
